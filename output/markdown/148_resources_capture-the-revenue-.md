# Capture the Revenue Your Billing System is Missing
**Abstract:**
You have a great subscription billing system, but it cannot complete large numbers of renewal payments due to bank errors that cause unnecessary declines. While your system has a built-in feature that can help recover some failed payments, FlexPay’s integrated recovery solution will supercharge your failed payment recovery results and deliver the full revenue potential your billing system can deliver.
**Why You Should Attend**
Unrecovered failed payment cause avoidable revenue losses and churn. This session will show you how upgrading your system’s recovery feature will:
  * Increase revenue and profits
  * Increase subscriber LTV
  * Reduce involuntary churn


**What You’ll Learn**
  * How to get more revenue from your billing system
  * Why a specialized solution will perform better than a built-in tool
  * How quick and easy the integration process actually is  
  



**Who should attend?**
✔️ Merchants using Stripe, Recurly, Chargebee, Zuora, Recharge, ROI Solutions, Vindicia, Aria, Kount
✔️ Anyone exploring smarter monetization through integrated services
